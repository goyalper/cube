Our Educational Philosophy

At CubeMaster Pro, we believe that learning should be fun, interactive, and accessible. Our approach combines the best of traditional teaching methods with modern technology to create an unparalleled learning experience.

The "Golden 20 Steps" methodology was developed by analyzing thousands of solving patterns used by world champion speed cubers. These 20 algorithms represent the core knowledge that solves 95% of all cube scenarios, making them the most efficient way to learn.
